How It Works

Sign Up

Create your account in minutes and set up your profile with your travel preferences.

Find or Offer

Search for available rides or offer your empty seats to others heading in the same direction.

Connect & Travel

Match with compatible travel partners, confirm details, and enjoy your shared journey.

Why Choose PoolMate?

Save Money

Split travel costs and reduce expenses on fuel, tolls, and parking.

Community

Connect with fellow students and make new friends during your journey.

Eco-Friendly

Reduce your carbon footprint by sharing rides and decreasing the number of vehicles on the road.

Convenience

Flexible scheduling and door-to-door service makes travel easier than public transportation.
